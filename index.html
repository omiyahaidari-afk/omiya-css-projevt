History of Our Creation

Calvin Klein, Inc. was established in New York in 1968 by Calvin Klein and Barry Schwartz with a small initial investment. The brand rose to prominence by accident when a buyer from Bonwit Teller placed a large order.

and continued to grow through provocative advertising for its minimalist jeans, underwear, and perfumes, becoming an iconic American brand known for simple, high-quality designs and worldwide distribution. PVH acquired the company in 2003.
